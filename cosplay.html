    What is Cosplay?

    Cosplay is a performance art where participants wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character.
    Cosplay applies to any costumed role-playing in venues or on stage. Favorite sources for participants to cosplay from:
  • Manga
  • Anime
  • Comic Books
  • Cartoons
  • Video Games
  • TV Series
  • Movies
  • Live-Action Films
    •  The hobby of Cosplay has been rapidly growing since 1990 all over the WORLD.
